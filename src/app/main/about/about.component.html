What is a go-show ?

Do you crave a captivating podcast? Do you need some beautiful video in your life? If so, you’re in luck! Packaged in a digital app for mobile devices, a new form known as the go-show combines videos and podcasts to tell one fascinating story.

What is Fire Bones About?

Amra Boustani, a Lebanese-American agri pilot who moonlights as a Pentecostal preacher, has mysteriously disappeared on a ferry flight to the Middle East. Folks in her hometown are left with heartbreak and confusion. A year later, a poet and a filmmaker from Dallas named Greg and Bart visit the Delta and get swept up in the mystery surrounding Amra’s disappearance. They discover that a local impresario has bought Amra’s old church, The Fire Bones Tabernacle, and converted it into an experimental-poetry salon. Greg and Bart get to know the colorful crew that hangs out there: a bunch of artists from the Delta countryside who finally have a place to party and collaborate on their crazy art. The Fire Bones scene is fractured by hurtful rumors, political polarization, and lingering anxieties about Amra’s disappearance, but a breakthrough seems possible when a boy YouTuber in Lebanon claims he knows what happened to Amra.
